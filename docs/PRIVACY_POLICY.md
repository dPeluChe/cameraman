# Privacy Policy — Cameraman

_Last updated: June 2026_

**Cameraman does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## Local-first by design

All recordings, projects, captions, and exported files are stored locally on
your Mac. No account is required to use the app, and the app works fully offline.

## Camera, microphone, and screen access

Cameraman accesses your camera, microphone, and screen **solely to perform screen
recording as requested by you**. This media is written only to your Mac and is
never uploaded, transmitted, or shared.

## On-device transcription

Speech-to-text transcription runs **entirely on-device** (Apple Silicon). The
audio used for transcription is never sent to any server.

## Software updates

Cameraman can check for new versions by querying the public GitHub Releases API
(`github.com/dPeluChe/cameraman/releases`). This request contains **no personal
information** — it only fetches the list of published releases.

## Automation (MCP server)

The optional built-in MCP server runs locally and lets AI assistant apps on your
Mac edit your projects. It does not transmit your projects anywhere; all
operations happen on your machine against your local project files.

## Data collected

**None.** Cameraman has no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that
collect data.
